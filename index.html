Snoopy's First Appearance

Snoopy made his debut in the Peanuts comic strip on October 4, 1950

but he wasn't initially the main character. At first, he was a minor dog who would just walk around, but as the strip progressed, he became more developed and gained personality.

Snoopy, the iconic beagle from the Peanuts comic strip created by Charles M. Schulz, has a rich history and many interesting aspects.

Snoopy’s charm and creativity continue to captivate people of all ages, and he’s a symbol of fun, imagination, and the joy of simply being yourself!
